GOODBYE 2012!

2012 was a great year for our newly born Duo - After opening the Jewish Film Festival in the Jerusalem Cinematheque in December, and participating in Amsterdam's International Jewish Music Festival in October, and after numerous concerts and radio concerts, we are about to start 2013 with a blast and participate in the KOL-2-5 project, where young Israeli musicians record a short analog session, in order to revive the LIVE feel music used to have in recording studios back in the days of tape. Come back and visit around Feb. for some goodies!
you can always send us a note via email and we will add you to our mailing list!
